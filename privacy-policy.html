Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

Quenchr ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Quenchr mobile application. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: email address and password when you register.
  • Screenshots and images: photos you submit for feed analysis. These are processed to generate a content score and are not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Scan history: your feed scores and scan results, stored to your account so you can track progress over time.
  • Usage data: app interactions used to improve the service.

2. Camera & Photo Access

Quenchr requests access to your camera and photo library solely to allow you to select or capture screenshots for scanning. Images are processed for content analysis and are not shared with third parties except as described below.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the feed scanning and scoring service.
  • To display your feed health history and progress.
  • To manage your account and subscription.
  • To improve the accuracy and performance of the app.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase – authentication and data storage. Privacy policy
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) – images may be sent to Anthropic's API for content analysis. Privacy policy
  • RevenueCat – subscription management. Privacy policy
  • Google Play / Apple App Store – app distribution and payment processing.

5. Data Retention

Scan results are stored to your account until you delete it. You may request full deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

Quenchr is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact us at: privacy@quenchr.app
